My Projects

Discover my projects, where creativity meets innovation

Design & Testing of a Mass Tuned Damper for Vibrational Control

Designed, simulated, and tested a mass-tuned damper for a one-story building using ANSYS and MATLAB, comparing theoretical, numerical, and experimental results.

Analysis of Aerodynamics and stall behavior of NACA 23018 using ANSYS

Compared the ANSYS model of NACA 23018 aerofoil with experimental results to analyze stall location, critical angle of attack, lift, drag, and pressure-velocity distribution.

Design Validation and Stress Analysis of a Wrecking Crane

Performed stress analysis for design validation of a wrecking crane, optimizing boom structure with I-beam and wrecking ball materials for structural integrity.

Four Bar Release-Latch Mechanism

Designed, 3D-printed, and analyzed a release latch mechanism, securing 3rd place in an inter-university design competition.

FEA of Composite Pressure Vessel

Performed finite element analysis on a Type-IV pressure vessel for liquid ethane storage at -60°C and 15 MPa, utilizing a ULDPE liner for cryogenic resilience and five-layered prepreg carbon fiber for structural strength.

FYP: Design & FEM Analysis of Type V Cryogenic Cylinders (-200°C)

Conducted research, FEA (ANSYS, Abaqus), design, and experimentation on composite cylinders for cryogenic applications. An industry-sponsored mega project with a planned research publication.
